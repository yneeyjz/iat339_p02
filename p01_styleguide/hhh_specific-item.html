Egg Linguine

$9.95

An elegant fine pasta with a high egg content. Made with a bronze dye to give a desirable rough finish. Slowly dried to produce an excellent quality pasta.



Buy

  • Ingredients

    Durum whete semolia, EGGS (31%)

    Allergy advice: for allergens, see ingredients in CAPITALS.

    Store sealed in a cool, dry place.

    Instructions

    Cook in a large pan of salted, boiling water for 5-7 minutes. This pasta is ideal with mushroom sauce or rich meaty ragu.
